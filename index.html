Research direction

What I work on

My research interests center on how large models encode and reason over spatial structure. I am especially interested in representation-centric approaches that connect generative latent structure, multimodal perception, and structured reasoning.

  • Spatial semantic understanding in large models
  • Multimodal representation learning for map-based and structured data
  • Spatiotemporal reasoning with graph-based or dynamic relational models
  • Human-centered AI systems with real-world usability constraints
What I build

How I approach projects

I work from problem framing to implementation: defining a concrete task, building a reproducible pipeline, evaluating limitations carefully, and packaging results into a clean repository or interactive prototype.

  • Research codebases with explicit datasets, methods, and outputs
  • Interactive prototypes with accessible UI and clear information flow
  • Project documentation designed for future reuse and review
